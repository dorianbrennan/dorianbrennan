Fantastic to see Rachel Entrekin win this year’s [Cocodona 250](https://www.aravaiparunning.com/cocodona/) outright. She smashed the previous course record, held by Dan Green, by more than two hours.

Coke and Donut starts in Black Canyon City, with runners finishing in Flagstaff over 400km away.
